products 製品について

音楽の風景を再現する

「音楽が表現するさまざまな風景を、生活へゆるやかにつなぐ感覚をもたらすもの」という想いを込め、sonihouseの3つのスピーカーにはそれぞれ「風景」という意味をあらわす言葉を名づけています。“scenery”“sight”そして“view”。アーティストたちが描く音を忠実に再現し、彼らが表現する風景へと誘います。小音量でも繊細な響きが空間全体に広がりながら、さらには部屋の外から微かに聴こえる虫の音や、鳥の声も同時に楽しめるように。風景を再現し、ひらく。そんな音の佇まいです。

Reproduction of the scenery of music

It is our wish that sonihouse speakers serve as something that gently connects many different sceneries described by the sound and scenes of our daily lives. Each of three speaker models sonihouse produces has a name related to the landscape: "scenery,” "sight” and "view." These products faithfully reproduce the sound drawn by artists and invite listeners to the scenery depicted by them. Enjoy delicate, even, low-volume sound that reaches every corner of a space, and yet listen to the singing of insects and birds faintly heard from outside the room at the same time. It is this clear reproduction and an invitation to scenic sound that we aim to offer.
